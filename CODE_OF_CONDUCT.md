# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

As contributors and maintainers, we are committed to ensuring that participation
in this project is a respectful, professional, and inclusive experience for everyone,
regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

We aim to foster an open and collaborative environment where all participants
are treated with courtesy and respect.

---

## Expected Standards of Behaviour

Examples of behaviour that contribute to a positive and productive environment include:

- Using respectful and inclusive language
- Being considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback in a professional manner
- Acting in good faith and with integrity
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its community

Unacceptable behaviour includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary conduct
- Abusive, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal attacks or inflammatory remarks
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Any behaviour that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for upholding these standards and may take
appropriate action in response to behaviour that is inconsistent with this Code
of Conduct.

This may include moderating discussions, requesting changes, or restricting
participation where necessary to protect the project and its contributors.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests,
discussions, and other interactions related to this repository.

---

## Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness behaviour that you believe breaches this Code of Conduct,
please report it confidentially to:

**InfoSec@BeYondWMS.com**

All reports will be reviewed promptly and treated with discretion.
The maintainers are committed to addressing concerns fairly and proportionately.

---

## Enforcement

Responses to breaches of this Code of Conduct will be proportionate to the circumstances
and may include:

1. A private request to modify behaviour
2. A formal warning
3. Temporary restriction of participation
4. Permanent removal from the project community
